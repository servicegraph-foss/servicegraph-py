# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or expertise.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and skill levels
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Assuming good intent and offering guidance without judgment
- Recognizing that everyone is learning and growing

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- Dismissive or condescending language about someone's skills or knowledge
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## A Judgment-Free Environment

This project is a **judgment-free zone**. We welcome contributors at all skill levels and recognize that everyone makes mistakes, asks questions, and learns at their own pace.

**What this means:**
- Questions are never "stupid" or "obvious"—if someone asks, it's worth answering respectfully
- Mistakes are learning opportunities, not character flaws
- Code review feedback focuses on the code and approach, never the person's abilities
- We assume good intent and offer constructive guidance
- Experience differences are expected and respected

**What we don't do:**
- Label work or questions as careless, lazy, or uninformed
- Make assumptions about someone's knowledge or attention to detail
- Use sarcasm or dismissive language in feedback
- Compare contributors to each other in diminishing ways

Everyone here is working to build something useful. Let's keep discussions focused on solutions and learning, not on judging effort or capability.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team at servicegraph.contact@zohomail.com

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
